HOW TO PLAY

GOAL: Keep the black Escalade alive. If the truck's health hits 0, it's over.

DESKTOP: WASD or arrows to move. Mouse to aim. Left-click to shoot. 1-5 or scroll wheel to switch weapons. SPACE to reload. SHIFT to dash.

iPHONE / TOUCH: Drag anywhere on the LEFT half of the screen to move — joystick follows your thumb. Fire button on the right; auto-aims at nearest enemy. Tap DASH to roll (invincible while dashing). Tap weapon icons to swap.

WEAPONS: Draco (rapid), Glock (semi-auto, fast reload), Boxing Gloves (melee, no ammo), RPG (explosive, slow), CRAB LASER (special - find it in wave 6+).

POWER-UPS: Drop from kills. Health (red cross), Speed (lightning), Damage (skull), Shield (blue), Ammo refill (yellow crate). They magnet to you when close.

CASH: Every kill drops coins. Pick them up — they magnet too. Each coin = +5 score.

COMBOS: Quick consecutive kills build a multiplier (up to ×5). Don't take damage or it resets!

MULTI-KILLS: Kills within 600ms cluster — DOUBLE / TRIPLE / MEGA / MASSACRE.

PERFECT WAVE: Clear a wave without taking damage for a bonus.

BOSSES: Wave 5 = Giant Crab. Wave 10 = 2Slimey. Wave 15 = Mirror 2X. Beat all three to win.
