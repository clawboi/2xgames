CRABCAGE 2X

protect the escalade. survive the swarm.

HOW TO PLAY

GOAL: Keep the black Escalade alive. If the truck's health hits 0, it's over.

DESKTOP: WASD or arrows to move. Mouse to aim. Left-click to shoot. 1-5 to switch weapons. SPACE to reload. SHIFT to dash.

iPHONE / TOUCH: Drag anywhere on the LEFT half of the screen to move — joystick follows your thumb. Fire button on the right; auto-aims at nearest enemy. Tap DASH to roll (invincible while dashing). Tap weapon icons to swap.

WEAPONS: Draco (rapid), Glock (semi-auto, fast reload), Boxing Gloves (melee, no ammo), RPG (explosive, slow), CRAB LASER (special - find it in waves).

POWER-UPS: Drop from kills. Health (red cross), Speed (lightning), Damage (skull), Shield (blue), Ammo refill (yellow crate). Walk near them — they pull toward you.

COMBO: Quick consecutive kills build a multiplier (up to ×5). Don't take damage or it resets!

BOSSES: Wave 5 = Giant Crab. Wave 10 = 2Slimey. Wave 15 = Mirror 2X. Beat all three to win, then it loops endless.
