HOW TO PLAY

GOAL: Keep the black Escalade alive. Survive 15 waves. Beat 3 bosses.

DESKTOP: WASD or arrows to move. Mouse aims. Left-click shoots. 1-7 or scroll wheel switches weapons. SPACE to reload. SHIFT to dash. E to emote.

iPHONE: Drag left half to move (joystick follows your thumb). FIRE button auto-aims at nearest enemy. DASH = invincible roll. EMOTE = dance.

WEAPONS: Draco, Glock, Fists (wider melee arc + forward dash), RPG (now explodes on impact OR mid-flight with massive screen shake), Crab Laser (wave 6+ pickup). SHOP unlocks: Sawed-off shotgun, Stun gun (sonic AOE).

POWER-UPS: Health, Speed, Damage, Shield, Ammo, Lightning (smites all in 220px), Slo-mo.

CASH: Drops from kills. Magnets to you. Carries between runs in your BANK. Spend in shop on weapon damage, truck HP, move speed, weapon unlocks.

WATCH OUT: Wave 8+ Crabs with guns. Wave 10+ Fans run at you for pics — SHOOT THEM AND YOUR GUN LOCKS FOR 5 SECONDS. Boss fights = no truck, you're in their world; dodge lightning shadows.

SUIT DUDE: When the Escalade's HP drops below half, a dude in shades pops out and starts firing at enemies.

BOSSES: Wave 5 = Giant Crab (underwater). Wave 10 = 2Slimey (mosh pit, 2X on stage). Wave 15 = Mirror 2X (caged in). Lightning strikes during boss fights — watch for shadow telegraphs and step OUT.

AFTER WAVE 15: Mirror 2X drops a CD. Pick it up → after-party. Drink (wavy vision), smoke (hazy slo-mo), bump into a girl dancer to take her home as your follower. Walk into the green EXIT door to continue into wave 16+.

FOLLOWER: The girl you took from the party trails you through later waves. She's there for the vibes.
