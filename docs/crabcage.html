HOW TO PLAY

GOAL: Keep the black Escalade alive. Survive 15 waves. Beat 3 bosses to reach the finale at wave 30.

DESKTOP: WASD or arrows to move. Mouse aims & left-click shoots. HOLD SPACE to auto-aim AND auto-fire. R reloads. SHIFT to dash. TAB to pause. Q activates stored power-up. 1-5 selects a power slot. E to emote.

iPHONE: Drag left half to move (joystick follows your thumb). FIRE button auto-aims & fires. DASH for slo-mo i-frames. Tap pause for inventory, shop, playlist.

WEAPONS (4 tiers): grey (Draco/Glock/Fists/RPG), green (Sawed-off, Stun, Crab Laser), gold (★ Gold Draco, ★ Gold RPG, ★ Plasma), red epic (⚡ Tesla = chain lightning, 🔥 Flamethrower = burn DOT).

POWERS (Q to fire): Shockwave, Nuke, Medpack, Time Warp. Buy at shop, store up to 5. Pick slot with 1-5 keys.

CASH: Drops from kills. Magnets to you. Carries between runs in your BANK. Shop unlocks reset on death — cash and high score stay.

WATCH OUT: Fans run for pics — shooting them locks your gun for 5 sec. Bosses dodge knockback to finish their moves.

BOSSES: Wave 5 = Giant Crab. Wave 10 = 2Slimey (charges if you camp far). Wave 15 = Mirror 2X. Then after-party at the CD pickup. Wave 30 = finale rematch.
